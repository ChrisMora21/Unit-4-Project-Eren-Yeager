About Me

Eren Yeager, a former member of the survey corps is a 19-year-old who lived in Shingansha District with his parents peacefully until the fall of Wall Maria when he witnessed his mother being eaten by a titan. And from that day on Eren grew intense hatred towards the tians and had one goal on his mind that will keep him running to the very end. That goal is to avenge his mother and kill all the remaining titans and enemies he meets along the way roaming on earth until nothing remains and he will do anything in order to get what he wants.
